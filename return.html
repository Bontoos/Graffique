Returns & Refunds Policy

Return Policy

At Graffique, we accept returns under the following conditions:

  • The original tags must still be attached to the clothing item
  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original condition
  • Returns must be initiated within 14 days of receiving your order
  • Original packaging must be intact

Important Notice:

Please note that we do not provide refunds. Store credit will be issued for approved returns.

Return Process

  1. Contact our customer service team at support@graffique.com
  2. Include your order number and photos of the item with tags attached
  3. Wait for return authorization from our team
  4. Ship the item back using tracked shipping
  5. Once received and inspected, store credit will be issued

Exceptions

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Items without original tags
  • Worn or washed items
  • Items marked as final sale
  • Customized or personalized items

For any questions regarding our return policy, please contact our customer service team at support@graffique.com
