Shipping Terms & Information - Graffique

Shipping Methods

We currently offer the following shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping (5-7 business days)
  • Express Shipping (2-3 business days)
  • International Shipping (10-15 business days)

Order Tracking

Tracking availability depends on the shipping carrier selected for your order. Once your order ships, you will receive:

  • A shipping confirmation email
  • Tracking number (where available)
  • Estimated delivery date

Please note that some shipping methods may not provide detailed tracking information.

Shipping Policies

  • Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days
  • Shipping times are estimates and may vary based on location
  • We are not responsible for delays caused by customs or weather conditions
  • Address changes cannot be made once an order has shipped
  • International customers are responsible for any customs fees
